Admission Procedure

Admission Procedure

  • 1. The list of selected candidates will be put up on the notice board at the coorporate office.
  • 2. The admission procedure will be intimated to the parents of the selected candidates. It is mandatory for both parents to be present to complete various admission formalities.
  • 3. For students seeking admission from Class I onwards, it is mandatory to submit Transfer Certificate (original) from the last school attended.
  • 4. Admission is strictly base on merit.
  • 5. The following certificates are to be submitted along with the admission form. (a) Genuine birth certificatess. (b) Transfer certificates (where applicable).
  • 6. Two passport size Colour Photograph (students) Parents photograph also.
  • 7. Report card/mark statement form the previous School.
  • 8. Students health record from a Registered doctor.
  • 9. Fees, once paid at the time of admission, are not refundable in any case except the caution money (refundable) in case of refundable.
  • 10. The management reserves the right to have the last word in all matters relating to admission.

Syllabi for admission test classes I to VII

The applicants respective class will be Examined from the syllabi of NCRT of the Preceding class i.e.a student seeking admission To class VII will be examined form the NCRT Syllabi of class VI.The subject of examination will be Maths, Hindi & English for class I to V; Maths Hindi , English and science for class VI to VII.
