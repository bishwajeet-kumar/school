About Us

There is immediately a sense that it is more than just a school Ours is a school where children rejoice in their childhood, learn as they grow and find the whole process a fun – filled enjoyable experience that enables them to rise to their maximum potential. Our students are encouraged to see every day as an opportunity, a chance to develop and grow and be a lifelong learner to justify the school motto Education for Life. Creativity flourishes through drama, music and arts programmes along with competitive sport in many areas including football, squash, basketball, tennis, athletics, archery, skating, swimming and badminton to name a few. Along with the pursuit of academic excellence, our thrust is on a Value – based education with core values such as compassion, humility, respect, integrity and discipline, helping your child evolve into a fine human being. Our endeavor is to foster the best of Indian values and cultivate a global outlook, hence conditioning citizens of the International who could easily fit in any culture like a log in the wheel.. In short, kumarprogressiveschool provides students with the capacity to be inspired to become the very best individual they can. Please do come and visit us at Kumar Progressive School, Lakhisarai. It would be our absolute pleasure to show you around the campus which will allow you to feel warmth and sense of belongingness.

Education

Kumar Progressive School is an institution dedicated to quality education where kids Learn, Play, Explore, Invent, Think, Socialize and Enjoy. The school believe that education transforms people and educated people transforms Society, Nation & Future. HFHS prepare its students for ever Competitive, Changing, Challenging and Complex world. School emphasis on the All-Round Development of the child – Sensorial, Motor, Intellectual, Linguistic, Emotional, Moral, Social, Aesthetic, Creative, Cognitive and Physical Development. We at HFHS endeavor to nourish our students to be enlightened, productive & responsible global citizens who excel in a diverse and multicultural society. In today's cosmopolitan world narrow, parochial mindset will not work. if one want to succeed one need to compete with all the competitors at the global and level of competitiveness decide the stature at global level.
